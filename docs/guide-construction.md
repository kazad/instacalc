# Guide: Construction Math

Instacalc can handle lengths written with feet and inches: 

*  `3' 5"`
* `3' 5 1/2"`
* `3 feet 5 inches`

Force a result into this format wtih `as tape` or `as tape measure`: 

* `200 inches as tape`
